Reversi


Reversi is a 1v1 board game where you and your rival put a tile each turn. The goal is to have more tiles than your opponent at the end of the game.

You can only put your tiles where you can capture at least one of your enemy's. You capture a tile by "sandwiching" rival tiles with yours. You will capture all the tiles in all possible directions: horizontally, vertically and diagonally.

If you're not able to put a tile, your turn will be automatically passed. If no one can put a tile, the game will end and the result will be decided by the current tiles placed on the board.
